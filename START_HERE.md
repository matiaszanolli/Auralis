## üéØ KEY DESIGN DECISIONS

### Why These Colors?
- **Soft Violet (#7366F0)**: Professional, not neon
- **Electric Aqua (#47D6FF)**: Energy for audio-reactive elements
- **Deep Navy (#0D111A)**: Premium, calming background

### Why This Aesthetic?
- Inspired by **Tidal** (premium design)
- Inspired by **FabFilter/Ozone** (audio plugin aesthetic)
- Inspired by **macOS/Windows** (OS elegance)

### Why Elevation Over Borders?
- More elegant, less cluttered
- Better for dark themes
- Natural visual hierarchy
- Works across all screen sizes

### Why These Components?
- Large album covers ‚Üí better UX on modern screens
- Glass effect ‚Üí premium, translucent feel
- JetBrains Mono ‚Üí technical credibility (audio industry standard)
- Spectrum visualizer ‚Üí audio-first interface
- Circular buttons ‚Üí modern, easy to hit on any device
